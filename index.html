EMPOWERING TESO WIDE REGION

At TESOPEDO we deliver sustainable and impact development in TESO wide region of 10 Districts of Amuria, Bukedea,Ngora, Kaberamaido, Katakwi, Kapelebyong, Kumi, Pallisa, Serere, and Soroti, estimating 5 Million people, or 1 million households, and will integrate the Diaspora constituency. through Buildinng Capacity, Farming Technologies, Commercialization, Extension service, Financial Literacy Inclusions and mentoring.

COOPERATIVES

Teso PEDO seeks to implement the role of cooperatives in Sustainable and impact development for all, in a focus on Agriculture. Cooperatives in Africa or Uganda were looked at as an engine for poverty reduction and employment creation

CASHEW PROJECT

Cashew is now being fronted on a national Presidential directive for very strong reasons of income potential and climate mitigation adaptation for the arid and semi arid regions of the country. The planting is therefore being endorsed because the Cashew value chain is easily the current most demanded and is highly valueable in the market. Current production levels are not near enough, even for the foreseeable future. TESO PEDO is leading in promoting planting and the value chain development including the formation of the industry cooperative

CHICKEN

The People and Economy of Teso region including external markets are familiar with Free range Chicken and its value as domestic food security and nutrition with an element of cash income due to its high liquidity The two aspects of our work on this value chain is to transit it to a full commercial value offering including being an enabler for subsequent investment plans.
